  • Ranked

    • - You need enough wins to go here. This area is for serious players. Rule breakers are punished harder. Try to raise your skill level by winning games here! Talismans and viewing other players stats are disabled here. Ranks are measured in Skill. After playing a game, the rank is calculated based on the role you had and the rank of other players. If the players you beat are higher rank, you will receive more than skill than if the players you beat are of a lower rank. If you lose, you lose more skill if you are beaten by lower rank players. Currently you have to pay 5 gold to enter a ranked game. It was implemented to reduce suicides and make winning more important.

    • Win as a villager: 10 Gold
    • Win as a werewolf: 20 Gold
    • Win as a Fool or Headhunter: 40 Gold
    • Win as a solo role: 60 Gold

  • Custom

    • - Choose the roles and even change some settings! A great way to maximize the fun level! Creating a custom game costs 2000 coins or 680 diamonds. On purchase, the player also unlocks a special outfit and receives 25 Roses. Buying custom games is a one time purchase only, meaning it won't cost anything to host a lobby after purchase. Players can join lobbies in this game mode for free. Additional roles can be bought for roses or coins. Players who bought custom games can host a game with any of the roles they have. They can choose to let any other player join their game or restrict their lobby by setting it to private, putting a password or player level requirements. The host can also kick other players from their lobby. Talismans can either be enabled or disabled, and roles can be hidden on death. Gameplay settings also allow the host to change the time length for night, day, and voting stage, from 1 second to 120 seconds. Settings also allow players to enable voice mode if they want. The host can start their game anytime they want. If the lobby has been filled with 16 players, it will automatically start.

    In-game Descrption:

    Create unlimited games, select which roles appear and host private game parties!


  • Sandbox

    • - This game mode is to test new game modes before they come to other game modes. New roles that need to be tested by players are first introduced in this mode. Many roles were first introduced to the game through Sandbox mode. Roles change every week or so, similar to Quick Games. Sandbox mode's official language is English. There are no separate servers for different languages. You might be warned if you do not speak English in these games.


  • Play with Friends

    • - Play with Friends allows you to have a private server with your friends. This game mode allows you to play with 15 friends. If you do not have enough players to start a game, the app will move you and your friends to a game, where you can still play together, just with other people. If you enable Invites for everybody, you allow your friends to invite their friends too. You can not play ranked with friends but sandbox is open.


  • Spectator

    • - In Spectator Mode, players can watch their friends' games, but cannot intervene in the game. They can, however, talk in the dead chat. To watch their friends' games, players can simply view their friends list and tap the popcorn icon next to any player's name that is in a game. You can also spectate some games by tapping on an eye icon that appears in the chat bar.

Game Phases

Each game has 3 phases, which repeat until the game is finished. Each phase has 30 seconds for voting. With the exception of the Day phase as there would also be a discussion phase.

  • Night

    • - The werewolves or killers should choose a player to kill, the sect leader or bandit should pick someone to convert and the zombies may select player(s) to bite. Powerful villagers can also use their abilities to protect players or even uncover a playerâ€™s role for example. Some roles has special abilities at night while others don't. There are features to kill, use other abilities or even revealing a players role.


  • Discussion

    • - The night has ended and it is time to discuss or tell the new information. You can vote to skip the discussion phase if there are only 8 players alive. If all other players vote too, the discussion will be skipped.


  • Voting

    • - The person who gets the most votes will get lynched and their role will be visible after dying. From the village team and found a werewolf? Vote them! From the werewolves or you are a killer? Try to not get lynched! You have a voting role? This is your chance! Hint: Before a game starts you are in the game lobby, but there is no need to get bored! Check the participating roles and their abilities in the role menu.
